Might

Push a cart, break a neck, pin an opponent, leap a trench, carry a body, bend a grate, utter a terrifying bellow. If you want to swing a sword at your enemies, then this is the stat for you.

Speed

The stat representing many things, including overland speed, initiative, and some effects of Dexterity. Shoot first, dodge a blow, evade pursuit, snatch a purse, catch a falling sword, rush over hot coals. A high speed lets you get the leg up on your opponent before they even have a chance to react.

Health

As the name suggests, this is the stat for how healthy you are. Resist poison, recover from wounds, tread water, go without food, run for sustained periods, hold your breath. A high health will let you stay in the battle for longer.

Wits

This stat covers how quick your character is able to come up with ideas on the fly. Get a good idea, recall info you should know, find your way through a maze, avoid getting swindled, reason through many options.

Senses

How perceptive your character is, covering everything like their vision, their smell and even supernatural occurrences that they should have no real reason knowing about. Spot lurkers or hidden doors, aim a precise blow, pick out a disguise, call a bluff, search a room, decipher an odd tome, eavesdrop.

Grace

Good manners and worldly ways. Impress nobles, procure a favor, take out a loan, move elegantly, break a fall, know the latest gossip, pick up a hobby, tell a good story.

Grit

Pluck and great resolve. Handle trauma, resist torture, jump back up after a hit, use folk remedies, MacGyver an improvised tool or weapon. This represents your character's ability to make the worst of a bad situation, that they have no real reason making better.

Faith

A bond with the wondrous unknowable forces. Heal ailments and wounds, stave off curses, banish evil spirits, render things holy, make protective auras, channel ancestors, call on totemic guides. This does not reference any specific deity, or even have to reference any at all. In this game, it is up to the tireless campaign referee to choose which gods are present in the universe, if any are at all.

Sway

Force of personality and diplomatic skill. Drive a bargain, beg for mercy, convert someone to a cause, pass off a lie, scare your enemies.

Sneak

Skullduggery and misdirection. Backstab, pick a pocket, hide in shadows, slide past guards, pick locks, remember a layout, leave no trace, move quickly.
